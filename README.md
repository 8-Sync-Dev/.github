### 🌟 About Us

**8 Sync Dev** is a forward-thinking technology education platform and community dedicated to exploring modern software development disciplines. We integrate Cutting-Edge **AI**, **3D Web Game** development, and **Blockchain** technologies to provide accessible and robust programming education for all. 

Our mission is to help learners cultivate logical thinking, sharpen problem-solving skills, and seamlessly adapt to rapidly growing fields in the tech landscape.

---

### 💻 What We Do

We offer free, comprehensive tutorials and resources on various platforms covering:
- **Core Languages**: C/C++, Python, Java, SQL, TypeScript
- **Advanced Concepts**: Data Structures & Algorithms, Object-Oriented Programming, Database Design
- **Modern Development**:
  - 🤖 **AI & Agents**: LangChain, Ollama, AI Agentic Workflows
  - ⛓️ **Web3 & Blockchain**: Solidity, Foundry
  - 🌐 **Full-Stack & UI**: React, FletUI, Web/Desktop/Mobile App Deployment
